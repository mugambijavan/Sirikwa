Welcome to Sirikwa Dairies & Gen PLC

Our main goal is to improve the living standards and the overall human development conditions through improving incomes and access to high quality milk.

Learn more

Sirikwa Dairies is a farmer owned dairy company aimed in bulking, chilling and marketing high quality milk and also providing dairy related services.

The companyâ€™s registered main office is located in Ziwa town 45 Kms to the west of Eldoret town through University of Eldoret. With time the company has expanded and opened up branches in Kiplombe (Uasin gishu county) and Likuyani (Kakamega county). Sirikwa collects milk from Uasin gishu, Kakamega, Elgeyo marakwet and Transzoia counties.

We aim torwards making SIRIKWA a leading high quality dairy company that will improve the economic status of farmers and welfare of our people and also improving the welfare of our dairy farmers by bulking, chilling and marketing of high quality dairy products and providing related services to our customers.
